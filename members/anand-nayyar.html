About

Biography

Dr. Anand Nayyar is a globally recognized researcher and educator with exceptional contributions to computer science and engineering. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Desh Bhagat University in 2017, specializing in Wireless Sensor Networks, Swarm Intelligence, and Network Simulation.

Currently serving as Professor, Scientist, Vice-Chairman (Research), and Director of the IoT and Intelligent Systems Lab at the School of Computer Science, Duy Tan University, Da Nang, Vietnam, Dr. Nayyar has established himself as a leading figure in international research.
