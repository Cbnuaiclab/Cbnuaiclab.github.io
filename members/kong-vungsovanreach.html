About

Postdoctoral Researcher specializing in applied computer vision and AI, with 7+ years in software development and 5+ years in ML, AI, and computer vision. His research focuses on medical image analysis, safety surveillance systems, and real-world AI applications in healthcare.

Research Focus

  • Vision-Language Integration: Combining Large Language Models with visual data for multimodal AI applications
  • Safety Surveillance Systems: Fall detection, helmet detection, and real-time video monitoring using computer vision

  • Super-Resolution Imaging: Enhancing medical image quality for improved diagnostic accuracy
  • Healthcare AI: AI-driven prognosis and risk prediction for clinical decision support
