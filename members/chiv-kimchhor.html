Research Focus

Chiv Kimchhor's research focuses on the cutting-edge intersection of quantum computing and machine learning, exploring how quantum algorithms can enhance computational capabilities for complex optimization and learning tasks.

  • Quantum Machine Learning: Applying quantum computing principles to machine learning algorithms
  • Deep Learning: Advanced neural network architectures and optimization techniques
  • Quantum Computing: Variational quantum algorithms and quantum circuit optimization

Research Interests

Her research explores the emerging field of quantum machine learning, where she investigates how quantum computing can revolutionize traditional machine learning approaches. She is particularly interested in variational quantum algorithms and their applications in optimization problems, as well as efficient quantum circuit design and transpilation techniques.

Collaboration with AICLab

As a collaborating researcher with AICLab at Chungbuk National University, Chiv Kimchhor brings expertise in quantum computing and deep learning to complement the lab's research in applied AI and computer vision. This collaboration bridges quantum computing research at Pukyong National University with applied AI initiatives at AICLab.
