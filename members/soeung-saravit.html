About

About

Postdoctoral Researcher at AI Convergence Lab (AICLab), Chungbuk National University, with over 9 years in software development and 5+ years specializing in machine learning, deep learning, and applied AI. His research focuses on multimodal AI systems, real-time abnormal action detection, computer vision, and vision-language models (VLM).

Areas

Research Focus

  • Multimodal Action Detection: Spatio-temporal modeling and vision-language reasoning for real-time abnormal human action detection (fall detection, fight identification, safety compliance)
  • Computer Vision: Object detection, pose estimation, image classification, and intelligent video surveillance systems
  • Vision-Language Models (VLM): Multimodal AI combining visual and language understanding for scene reasoning and document intelligence
  • Large Language Models (LLM): Applied LLM systems including OCR-based document extraction, NLP pipelines, and prompt-driven solutions
  • IoT & Big Data Infrastructure: End-to-end IoT data pipelines for industrial sensor data collection, processing, storage, and monitoring using Spark and Kafka
  • Applied AI Systems: Address verification with geocoding and deep learning, blockchain-based voting systems, and real-world AI deployment
