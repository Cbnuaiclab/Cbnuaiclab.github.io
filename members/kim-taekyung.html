Areas

Research Areas

5 topics

Artificial Intelligence

  • Anomaly detection (deep learning/autoencoders; time-series and sensor data)
  • Computer vision for object detection and video understanding (object hierarchy/relationship extraction)
  • Pose-estimation-based action recognition and video fall detection
  • LLM reliability and hallucination analysis
  • LLM-based natural language → SQL generation (NL-to-SQL), data augmentation, and prompt-based performance improvement
4 topics

Big Data

  • Data analysis and modeling in distributed environments (power/IoT, etc.)
  • Federated-learning-based training and anomaly detection
  • Security-data-based attack detection (DDoS) and mitigation modeling
  • Data-driven predictive modeling (traffic/time-series forecasting)
4 topics

Quantum Computing

  • Optimization issues in NISQ environments (qubit allocation/mapping)
  • Use of quantum machines for solving combinatorial optimization problems (including surveys)
  • Classification of QAOA error-mitigation strategies and research on noise robustness
  • Applications of quantum–classical hybrid models (e.g., time-series/financial prediction)
