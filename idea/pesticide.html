The Agricultural Trilemma

Farmers face three critical challenges that cause financial loss and safety hazards. Hover over each card to understand the depth of the problem.

1. The Compliance Gap

Farmers often use familiar chemicals without realizing they are banned for specific harvest timelines.

Consequence: Discarded crops & fines.

2. Inventory Mismatch

Driving 30 minutes to a cooperative only to find the recommended product is out of stock.

Consequence: Wasted time & untreated pests.

3. Dosage Errors

Inefficient calculation leads to over-purchasing or under-diluting chemicals.

Consequence: Soil contamination & money waste.

AI Engine (RAG)

We utilize a Retrieval Augmented Generation architecture to prevent "hallucinations".

  • Retrieval: Searches Knowledge Base for pest treatments.
  • Augmentation: Cross-references with Inventory Data to find in-stock brands.
  • Generation: Constructs a natural language response with price and dosage.
